Our Story

About LeBaron

From a single-room clinic in Srinagar to a multi-city aesthetic destination — a journey built on vision, expertise, and trust.

2019 Founded in Srinagar
The Beginning

A Vision for World-Class Care, Close to Home

LeBaron Aesthetics & Dental Lounge was founded in 2019 by Dr. Syeda Tasneefa with a simple but powerful belief: patients in Srinagar and Delhi deserve the same standard of aesthetic and dental care available in Dubai, London, or Mumbai — without having to travel thousands of kilometers.

What began as a single consultation room has grown into a multi-specialty clinic with two premium branches, a team of six specialists, and a reputation for results that speak for themselves.

"I wanted to create a space where clinical excellence and human warmth coexist — where every patient feels heard, respected, and genuinely cared for."

— Dr. Syeda Tasneefa, Founder

Our Journey

Key Milestones

The path from a single room to a multi-city destination.

2019

LeBaron Founded

Dr. Tasneefa opens the first clinic in Srinagar, offering dermatology and basic aesthetic treatments.

2020

Dental Division Launched

Expansion into cosmetic and restorative dentistry with the addition of Dr. Almas Syed to the team.

2021

Hair Restoration Program

Introduction of FUE hair transplant and PRP therapy, establishing LeBaron as a regional leader in hair restoration.

2022

Advanced Aesthetics Suite

Investment in medical-grade laser platforms, advanced injectables, and body contouring technology.

2023

Delhi Branch Opens

Launch of the Safdarjung Enclave branch in South Delhi, bringing LeBaron's standards to the capital.

2024

Permanent Makeup Studio

Addition of microblading, lip blush, and permanent eyeliner services with internationally certified artists.
