Our Story

Home/About Us
"Beauty is a partnership."

Founded in 2019 by Dr. Syeda Tasneefa and Mohammad Yaseen, LeBaron Aesthetics & Dental Lounge was born from a shared belief: that world-class aesthetic and dental care should be accessible, personal, and transformative.

What began as a vision in Delhi has grown into a trusted name across North India. In 2024, we opened our Srinagar branch at Wani Complex, Munawarabad — bringing the same standards of excellence to Kashmir.

Our mission is simple: personalized, cutting-edge beauty solutions delivered with compassion. We do not believe in cookie-cutter treatments. Every procedure is a doctor-patient partnership, tailored to your individual needs and anatomy, for results that look beautifully natural.

From advanced hair restoration and skin aesthetics to precision dental care, LeBaron is where science meets artistry — and where you begin your transformation.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every patient like family. Your comfort, trust, and wellbeing are at the heart of everything we do.

Precision

We continuously adopt the latest technology and techniques from global aesthetic medicine to deliver superior outcomes.
